About Us



We are a local lifesaving club that teaches Rookies Lifegaurd awards for ages 7-12 and National Lifesaving Academy Awards for ages 13+.


We train at Aston-Cum-Aughton leisure centre on friday evenings 19:00-20:15.


If you interested in taking part please feel free to come along to the pool or get in touch through our contacts page.

Basic Water Safety


Learn basic water safety skills and stay safe in and around water

RLSS UK


We work to the Royal Life Saving Society guidelines to provide you with the highest level of learning.
